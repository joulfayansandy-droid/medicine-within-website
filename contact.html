Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

What should I include in my email?

Please let me know which offering you're interested in (Kambo, Temple Nights, Retreats, or 1:1 Coaching), any questions you have, and what you're hoping to explore or heal.

How quickly will I hear back?

I typically respond within 2-3 business days. For urgent matters, please note it in your subject line.

Can I book events directly?

Yes! Most events can be booked directly through our events page via Hipsy. For retreats and 1:1 work, please reach out via email or book a discovery call.

Location

Based in Amsterdam

Most events and sessions take place in Amsterdam, Netherlands. Retreats are held at beautiful nature locations within 2 hours of the city.

Exact locations are shared upon booking to maintain the privacy and sacredness of our spaces.
