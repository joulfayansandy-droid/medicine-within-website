Welcome to the sacred playground

Temple is a mirror to your life

Do you dare feel your presence, your truth, your desires, your curiosities — and meet who you truly are?

Sunday Tantrik Temple Day is a trauma-informed, consent-rooted, sovereign space shaped by presence, authenticity, curiosity, prayer, and play. Beginners and experienced practitioners, singles and couples, the curious, the tender, the fiery — all enter as they are, because every expression of life belongs here.

Nothing is forced. Nothing is performed. Nothing is sexualized. You meet yourself, you meet others, and something older than language moves through the room.

The lineage behind the temple

Before workshops… there were temples

Temple arts come from ancient tantrik, priestess, and shamanic traditions — lineages that understood transformation as embodied initiation.

  • The body as lived prayer
  • Ritual, song, breath, and devotion
  • Eros as healing: wild and authentic
  • Presence as medicine; spirit moving through the body
  • Humans remembering who they are through each other

Sacred Agreements

House Rules

These are the foundations that allow us to go deep together.

Consent is Everything

Every practice is an invitation, never an obligation. "No" is a complete sentence. We ask before touching. We honor each woman's boundaries without question.

What Happens in Temple, Stays in Temple

Confidentiality is sacred. Stories shared in circle are not repeated outside. No photos or recordings without explicit permission.

Come Sober

Temple is a drug and alcohol-free space. We get high on breath, movement, and connection—no substances needed.

Show Up Fully

Arrive on time. Put your phone away. Be present with yourself and the women around you. This is your time to drop in.
