Atmospheric Treatment

The Media Filter

To maintain the "Sacred & Alchemical" feel, all media should undergo a subtle atmospheric treatment:

  • • SEPIA (0.2): Warms the skin tones and pulls images into the "Bone & Gold" palette.
  • • CONTRAST (1.1): Deepens the shadows (Deep Teal) to create mystery.
  • • BRIGHTNESS (0.8): Lowers digital "glare" for a candlelit, intimate feel.
