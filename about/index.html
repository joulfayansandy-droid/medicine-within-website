About Sandi

Kambo practitioner, embodiment guide, and founder of Medicine Within.

The Journey

From Numbness to Aliveness

I'm Sandi—Kambo practitioner, embodiment guide, and founder of Medicine Within. I'm also a woman who's walked this path myself. I was successful on the outside but numb on the inside, until I found my way back through Kambo, Tantra, and embodiment work.

My journey taught me that you cannot truly feel pleasure if your system is blocked. That's why I created Medicine Within—a complete path from purification to pleasure, where we clear what blocks you and awaken what's been dormant.

Now I guide other women home to themselves, combining the medicine of Kambo, the wisdom of Tantra, and the power of embodiment practices. I hold space for women who are ready to stop performing and start living—to feel fully alive in their bodies again.

"I believe every woman has medicine within her. My job is to help you find it."

The Path of the Medicine

I walk the path between the Earth and the Fire. As a guide, I don't just share theoretical knowledge—I share what I have lived and integrated. My work is rooted in deep respect for the lineages I have been initiated into and the wisdom of the body.

Whether through the physical purification of Kambo or the soul-stirring rituals of the Temple, my intention is always the same: to help you remember who you are when you stop trying to be who the world expects you to be.
