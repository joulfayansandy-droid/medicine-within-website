About Sandi

Kambo practitioner, embodiment guide, and founder of Medicine Within.

The Woman Behind Medicine Within

Following the Mystery

I have always followed the mystery—in the same way nature moves. Instinctively. Cyclically. Without apology.

I was a wild child. I grew into a wild woman. And now, I stand as a wild queen—not wild as chaotic, but wild as nature itself.

By wild, I mean this: I listen. I follow what moves beneath logic. I trust the deeper current—even when my mind doesn't yet understand it. This has been the guiding thread of my life.

The Shift

From Mind to Embodiment

I arrived to this work through embodiment. Through living in my body. Through crossing thresholds. Through sitting in temples, ceremonies, initiations, and long nights of truth. Through learning when to open—and when to slow down.

My formal education began in the mind: a Bachelor's in Communication Arts and a Master's in Advertising. I was trained to understand psychology, narrative, perception, desire—how humans think, choose, attach, and respond.

And yet, something deeper was calling. In 2018, my life shifted decisively. Just before my first Ayahuasca ceremony, I sat with Kambô—and that encounter changed everything.

The medicine met me in layers I didn't yet have language for. It spoke to my body. It cleared something ancient. It restored a sense of inner authority I had forgotten.

The Awakening

Meeting the Feminine

Around the same time, something else revealed itself. The Feminine—not as an aesthetic, not as an identity, but as a living force inside me.

I saw clearly how parts of me had learned to survive in structure and control—how my depth, eros, and sensitivity had gone into hiding because they didn't feel safe to exist in the world as they were.

And when she began to emerge, she didn't come alone—she came with archetypes:

  • ✧ The Wild Woman
  • ✧ The Tantrika
  • ✧ The Queen
  • ✧ The Devotional One
  • ✧ The Fierce Protector
  • ✧ The Mystic

This didn't start a career. It began a soul journey.

The Vision

What I Care About

I care about integration—living the Temple Life. About what happens after the opening. About how truth becomes lived. I am not interested in peak experiences that leave people fragmented.

I am interested in paths that:

  • • Build emotional maturity
  • • Deepen relational capacity
  • • Strengthen nervous system resilience
  • • Bring spirituality into daily life
  • • Allow eros to exist without domination or collapse

The Resonance

Who I Work With

I work with humans who are done with surface-level spirituality and feel the pull toward depth, truth, and embodied presence.

Those who:

  • • Are curious about eros but no longer want chaos
  • • Value ritual and discernment
  • • Want to live awake—not just visit altered states
  • • Are ready for the real, raw, unapologetic life

There is no hierarchy here. Only readiness.
