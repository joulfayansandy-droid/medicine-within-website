Absolute Contraindications

If any of these apply, you cannot receive Kambô.

  • Serious heart conditions, heart surgery, pacemakers, or stents
  • History of stroke or brain hemorrhage
  • Blood pressure medication or blood thinners
  • Epilepsy or severe mental illness (psychosis, bipolar)
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding (first 6 months)
  • Recent major surgery (last 2-3 months)
  • Under 18 years of age
  • Water fasting 24h before (dangerous)
  • Alcohol or drugs within 24h of ceremony

Relative Contraindications

These require prior communication and a case-by-case assessment:

  • Antidepressants / SSRIs
  • Anxiety or sleep medication
  • Thyroid or diabetes medication
  • Asthma medication
  • Trauma history requiring specific modification
  • Panic disorder or acute grief

Preparation

24 hours before:

  • Eat clean, light food
  • No alcohol or recreational drugs
  • Hydrate gently and sleep well

Morning of:

  • No coffee or caffeine
  • No fasting (eat a light meal 3-4h before)

Aftercare

Integration is key:

  • Rest and allow the medicine to settle
  • Hydrate and eat light, nourishing food
  • Avoid alcohol for 24-48 hours
  • Spend time in nature or stillness
  • Journaling to capture insights

Safety FAQ

Is Kambô safe?

Yes — when properly screened and facilitated by a trained practitioner. Your safety is my absolute priority.

Will I vomit?

Maybe. Purge can be physical, emotional, energetic, or subtle. Every experience is unique.

How long does it last?

The entire ceremony container is 2-3 hours, including preparation and rest.

Can I come if I'm scared?

Yes. Fear is a natural response to the unknown. We move gently and at your pace.
