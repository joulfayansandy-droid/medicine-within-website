Absolute Contraindications

If any of these apply, you cannot receive Kambô.

  • Serious heart conditions
  • Heart surgery, pacemaker, stents
  • Stroke history
  • Blood pressure medication
  • Epilepsy
  • Pregnancy
  • Breastfeeding (first 6 months)
  • Recent major surgery
  • Under 18 years old
  • Severe mental illness (psychosis, bipolar)
  • Water fasting 24h before (dangerous)
  • Alcohol or drugs within 24h

If unsure → info@medicinewithin.nl

Relative Contraindications (Case-by-Case)

These require prior communication:

  • Antidepressants / SSRIs
  • Anxiety or sleep medication
  • Thyroid medication
  • Diabetes
  • Asthma medication
  • Trauma history requiring modification
  • Panic disorder or acute grief

Safety Protocols

  • Trauma-informed facilitation
  • Hydration monitored
  • Consent-first container
  • Optional Rapé & Sananga
  • No forced purging
  • Nothing sexualised
  • Integration guidance

Preparation

24 hours before:

  • Eat clean, light food
  • No alcohol or recreational drugs
  • Hydrate gently and sleep well

Morning of:

  • No coffee or caffeine
  • No fasting
  • Light meal 3-4h before

Aftercare

Integration is key:

  • Rest and allow the medicine to settle
  • Eat light, nourishing food
  • Hydrate well
  • Avoid alcohol for 24-48 hours
  • Spend time in nature, journaling or stillness

Safety FAQ

Is Kambô safe?

Yes — when properly screened and facilitated by a trained practitioner. Your safety is my absolute priority.

Will I vomit?

Maybe. Purge can be physical, emotional, energetic, or subtle. Every experience is unique.

How long does it last?

The entire ceremony container is 2-3 hours, including preparation and rest.

Can I come if I'm scared?

Yes. Fear is a natural response to the unknown. We move gently and at your pace.

Is Rapé or Sananga required?

No. They are always optional and offered only if they serve your process.
