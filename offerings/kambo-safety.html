Preparation

Preparing for your Journey

Safety starts with preparation. To ensure a safe and deep experience, please follow these guidelines:

  • Fast: No food for at least 8 hours before ceremony.
  • Hydration: Drink normally until arrival. In ceremony, you will drink a specific amount of water as guided.
  • Diet: Eat light and clean for 24-48 hours before (low salt, low spice, no heavy fats).
  • Substances: No alcohol, recreational drugs, or heavy medication for at least 24 hours.
