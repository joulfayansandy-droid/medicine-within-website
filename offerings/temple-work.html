The Roots

The Lineage Behind the Temple

Temple arts arise from ancient tantrik, priestess, and shamanic lineages — traditions that understood transformation as embodied initiation. Before self-help, before workshops, before modern tantra… There were temples.

  • The body as lived prayer
  • Ritual as a language of truth
  • Eros as healing and sacred
  • Community as initiation
  • Presence as medicine
  • Spirit moving through the body

Tantrik Temples are a modern rebirth of these lineages — rooted in sovereignty, relational intelligence, trauma-awareness, and your actual lived humanity.

This is not a performance space. Not a sexualised container. Not a place to “do tantra right.” Tantrik Temples are a living field shaped by presence, authenticity, curiosity, prayer, and play.
