Sacred Secretion

What is Kambô?

Kambô is the sacred secretion of the Phyllomedusa bicolor frog, traditionally used for cleansing, protection, vitality, and energetic alignment. It is non-psychedelic and works directly through the body’s intelligence.

Kambô acts through:

  • The lymphatic & immune systems
  • The nervous system
  • The emotional body
  • The energetic field
  • Intuitive and instinctual layers

Kambô does not “take you somewhere.” It brings you back into your body, your truth, and your strength.

Grounded Presence

The Sweetest Temple Assistant — Kaya

Kaya, my 4-year-old Yakutian Laika, is often present during ceremonies. She has been with me through every phase of my Kambô journey and intuitively understands the rhythm, silence, and gravity of this work.

She greets you warmly at the door — and once ceremony begins, she instinctively steps back, holding grounded presence from the edges of the field. At the end, she often returns — for a gentle check-in, quiet companionship, or soft cuddles.
