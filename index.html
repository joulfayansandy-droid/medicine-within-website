Meet Sandi

Your Guide Home

I am an Embodied Tantra guide, Temple facilitator, and Kambô practitioner — a woman devoted to the wild and the sacred, and to the alchemy that happens when both meet in the body.

I work with humans who are done living on the surface. Those who feel the pull toward depth, presence, raw honesty, wild sacred expression, and erotic liberation — the kind of intimacy that asks something real of you.

My lineage weaves tantric embodiment, ritual arts, archetypal work, shamanic pathways, and the Radiance Sutras tradition. This work transforms from the inside out — not by adding more, but by stripping away everything that isn’t you.

"A permission slip back to the parts of you that have been waiting to feel radiantly alive again. Unapologetic. Uncompromising. Real."
