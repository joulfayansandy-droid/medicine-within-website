1:1 Private Work

Some moments in life don't want fixing. They want to be met.

Some openings are too tender, too charged, or too specific for group spaces. They need slowness. Precision. Presence.

My 1:1 work exists for exactly this reason. Not to analyze you. Not to give advice. Not to make you "better."

But to walk with you while what has opened integrates into your body, your nervous system, and your lived truth. This is private ceremonial work — grounded, embodied, relational, and deeply human.

Activation vs Integration

How This Work is Different

Group Spaces

Group spaces activate. They mirror and open the field, creating collective energy and shared experience.

Private Work

Private work integrates. It digests and anchors the transformation into your body, relationships, and daily life.

This is why many people come 1:1 after Temple or Kambô, during relationship transitions, or when something needs precision rather than stimulation.

Integration here is not an afterthought. It is the core value of the work.
