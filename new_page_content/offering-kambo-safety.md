# CONTRAINDICATIONS, SAFETY & FAQ
## Kambô Safety & Contraindications
Required Reading Before Booking
Kambô is powerful, safe, and deeply healing when guided correctly.
Your safety comes first — always.

### ABSOLUTE CONTRAINDICATIONS
If any apply, you cannot receive Kambô:
serious heart conditions
heart surgery, pacemaker, stents
stroke history
blood pressure medication
epilepsy
pregnancy
breastfeeding (first 6 months)
recent major surgery
under 18 years old
severe mental illness (psychosis, bipolar)
water fasting 24h before (dangerous)
alcohol or drugs within 24h
If unsure → info@medicinewithin.nl

### RELATIVE CONTRAINDICATIONS (CASE-BY-CASE)
These require prior communication:
antidepressants / SSRIs
anxiety or sleep medication
thyroid medication
diabetes
asthma medication
trauma history requiring modification
panic disorder or acute grief

### SAFETY PROTOCOLS
trauma-informed facilitation
hydration monitored
consent-first container
optional Rapé & Sananga
no forced purging
nothing sexualised
integration guidance

### PREPARATION
24 hours before:
eat clean
no alcohol or drugs
hydrate gently
sleep well
Morning of:
no coffee
no fasting
light meal 3–4h before

### AFTERCARE
rest
light food
hydration
avoid alcohol 24–48h
journal, nature, stillness

→ Book an Integration Session

### FAQ
Is Kambô safe?
Yes — when properly screened and facilitated.
Will I vomit?
Maybe. Purge can be physical, emotional, energetic, or subtle.
How long does it last?
2–3 hours including rest.
Can I come if I’m scared?
Yes. Fear is common. We move gently.
Is Rapé or Sananga required?
No. Always optional.
