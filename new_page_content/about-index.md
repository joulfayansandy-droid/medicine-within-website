# ABOUT SANDI
## The Woman Behind Medicine Within
I have always followed the mystery-
in the same way nature moves.
Instinctively. Cyclically. Without apology.
I was a wild child.
I grew into a wild woman.
And now, I stand as a wild queen —
not wild as chaotic, but wild as nature itself.
By wild, I mean this:
I listen.
I follow what moves beneath logic.
I trust the deeper current — even when my mind doesn’t yet understand it.
This has been the guiding thread of my life.

## FROM MIND TO EMBODIMENT
I arrived to this work through embodiment.
Through living in my body.
Through crossing thresholds.
Through sitting in temples, ceremonies, initiations, and long nights of truth.
Through learning when to open — and when to slow down.
My formal education began in the mind:
a Bachelor’s in Communication Arts
and a Master’s in Advertising.
I was trained to understand psychology, narrative, perception, desire —
how humans think, choose, attach, and respond.
And yet, something deeper was calling.
In 2018, my life shifted decisively.
Just before my first Ayahuasca ceremony, I sat with Kambô —
and that encounter changed everything.
The medicine met me in layers I didn’t yet have language for.
It spoke to my body.
It cleared something ancient.
It restored a sense of inner authority I had forgotten.
I didn’t decide to work with medicine.
I was called.
I asked the facilitator to teach me — she felt the pull too, and said yes.
From there, spirit began arranging the path through resonance.

## MEETING THE FEMININE
Around the same time, something else revealed itself.
The Feminine —
not as an aesthetic, not as an identity,
but as a living force inside me.
I saw clearly how parts of me had learned to survive in structure and control —
how my depth, eros, and sensitivity had gone into hiding
because they didn’t feel safe to exist in the world as they were.
My feminine was too alive.
Too instinctual.
So she waited.
And when she began to emerge, she didn’t come alone —
she came with archetypes.
The Wild Woman.
The Tantrika.
The Queen.
The Devotional One.
The Fierce Protector.
The Mystic.
This didn’t start a career.
It began a soul journey.

## MY LINEAGE IS LIVED
My work is shaped by years of devotion to:
embodied tantra and tantrik ritual
temple facilitation (mixed and women-only spaces)
archetypal embodiment and transpersonal psychology
shamanic ritual arts and ceremonial space-holding
Kambô and soft plant allies
nervous-system-aware facilitation and integration
To me, lineage is
How someone holds the room.
How they listen.
How they pace truth.
How they meet vulnerability.
How they don’t rush what needs time.
My sacred spaces are known for being:
deeply safe without being flat
erotically alive
devotional without being dogmatic
playful without being careless
precise without being rigid
This work doesn’t force catharsis.
It listens.

## WHAT I CARE ABOUT
I care about integration- living the Temple Life.
About what happens after the opening.
About how truth becomes lived.
About how eros becomes intelligence instead of chaos.
About how intimacy becomes honest rather than addictive.
About how ritual doesn’t stay mystical — but becomes practical.
I am not interested in peak experiences that leave people fragmented.
I am interested in paths.
Paths that:
build emotional maturity
deepen relational capacity
strengthen nervous system resilience
bring spirituality into daily life
allow eros to exist without domination or collapse
This is why my work is structured the way it is:
Group spaces to open the field
Private work to integrate
Ongoing containers for those who feel the long arc

## WHO I WORK WITH
I work with humans who:
are done with surface-level spirituality
feel the pull toward depth, truth, and embodied presence
are curious about eros but no longer want chaos
value ritual and discernment
want to live awake — not just visit altered states
are ready for the real, raw, unapologetic life
Some arrive through temples.
Some through Kambô.
Some through 1:1 work.
Some arrive quietly, already knowing.
There is no hierarchy here.
Only readiness.
