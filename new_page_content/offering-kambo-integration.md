## WHEN PEOPLE SEEK KAMBÔ INTEGRATION
People often book integration when:
• Emotional material surfaced (grief, anger, tenderness, relief)

• There is clarity — but also questions
• The nervous system feels sensitive, raw, or wide open

Kambô works across many layers at once.

## WHAT A KAMBÔ INTEGRATION SESSION IS
A Kambô Integration Session is a private, embodied ritual space
to meet what the medicine activated — gently, consciously, and without urgency.
We listen to:
• how the cleanse affected your body and energy
• what emotional or psychological patterns were touched
• what wants rest, movement, expression, or boundaries
• how your system is reorganizing post-ceremony
• how the insights want to translate into daily life
This is not medical advice.
It is somatic, tantric, and shamanic integration — grounded in presence and respect for the intelligence of the medicine.
