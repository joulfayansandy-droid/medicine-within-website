### WHAT IS KAMBÔ?
Kambô is the sacred secretion of the Phyllomedusa bicolor frog, traditionally used for cleansing, protection, vitality, and energetic alignment.
It is non-psychedelic and works directly through the body’s intelligence.
Kambô acts through:
the lymphatic system
the immune system
the nervous system
the emotional body
the energetic field
intuitive and instinctual layers
Kambô does not “take you somewhere.”
