## A NOTE ON HOW I WORK
I don’t rush people into decisions.
I don’t push anyone into experiences they’re not ready for.
And I don’t hold conversations that bypass the body.
If you’re reaching out, take a breath first.
Notice what’s actually asking to be met.
That listening already begins the work.
