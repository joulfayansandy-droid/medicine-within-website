Temple arts arise from ancient tantrik, priestess, and shamanic lineages —
traditions that understood transformation as embodied initiation.
Before self-help.
Before workshops.
Before modern tantra…
There were temples.
Spaces where:
the body was lived prayer
ritual was a language of truth
eros was healing, wild, and sacred
community was initiation

Deep in your body, your heart, your nervous system —
you remember this.
